# Code of conduct

## TL;DR

-   FOSS Community United is committed to providing a respectful and harassment-free workspace for all members.
-   Discrimination or harassment based on race, gender, sexual orientation, religion, disability, or any other characteristic is not tolerated.
-   Members are expected to be respectful, professional, and considerate in their interactions with others.
-   Offensive or inappropriate behavior, including but not limited to verbal or physical abuse, will not be tolerated.
-   Misuse of the community resources will not be tolerated.
-   Violations of the code of conduct may result in consequences, including expulsion from the community or event.

## Introduction

FOSS Community United is dedicated to creating a welcoming and inclusive community for individuals interested in free and open-source software. We strive to create an environment where people feel comfortable learning, sharing ideas, and collaborating with one another. As a member of FOSS Community United, we ask that you abide by the following code of conduct to maintain a positive and supportive community.

## Expected Behavior

-   **Respectful and Inclusive Language**: FOSS Community United welcomes individuals from all backgrounds, cultures, and experiences. We ask that you use respectful and inclusive language in all interactions with others.
-   **Open-Mindedness**: FOSS Community United is a place for discussion and exploration of ideas. We encourage open-mindedness and the willingness to learn from others.
-   **Collaboration**: FOSS Community United is built on the foundation of collaboration. We value cooperation and teamwork in all our endeavors.
-   **Constructive Criticism**: We welcome constructive criticism and feedback, as long as it is given respectfully and with the intent to improve.
-   **Professionalism**: FOSS Community United expects all members to behave professionally in all interactions with others, both online and offline.
-   **Cleanliness**: While eating food is allowed, members are expected to clean up after themselves and maintain a clean workspace.
-   **Personal Belongings**: Members are expected to take care of their personal belongings and not leave them unattended.
-   **Sleeping in Cabin**: If you are a hostler, sleeping in the cabin is not allowed.
-   **Keys**: Keys to the workspace can only be handled by the maintainer.
-   **Putting items back in place**: Members are expected to put chairs or other items back in their place after use.
-   **Collaborative Work**: Work in collaboration with other members.
-   **Proper Use of Workspace**: Entertainment and other activities should only take place during their allotted time and should not disrupt other members. Bunking the classes and sitting in the workspace is not allowed.

## Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated in FOSS Community United:

-   **Harassment**: FOSS Community United has a zero-tolerance policy for harassment or ragging of any kind, including but not limited to verbal or written comments related to gender, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, religion, disability, or physical appearance.
-   **Discrimination**: FOSS Community United is an inclusive community that values diversity. Discrimination based on gender, sexual orientation, race, ethnicity, religion, disability, or physical appearance will not be tolerated.
-   **Intimidation**: We do not tolerate any behavior that is intended to intimidate, harass, or threaten others.
-   **Inappropriate Language**: FOSS Community United has a zero-tolerance policy for the use of inappropriate language, including but not limited to profanity or derogatory language.
-   **Disrespectful Behavior**: We expect all members to treat each other with respect and to refrain from any behavior that is disrespectful, harmful, or disruptive.
-   **Misuse of the Workspace**: Sitting in the workspace during class timings is not allowed except for the permission of the HoD.

## Enforcement

If a member of FOSS Community United engages in behavior that violates this code of conduct, the following steps will be taken:

-   The member will be notified of the violation and given a warning.
-   If the behavior persists, the member may be suspended or banned from FOSS Community United.
